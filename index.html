About

We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give.

Giving back to society is in fact
vague and misty in nature


We are here to help you to find the right way to help others so that your hard earned money is in the right hands. We will help you to make all your doubts go away and so that you can donate with confidence. To make it convinient and easy for donors, we have provided the different platforms for donations and different activites so that you can join us to change the world for impecunious lives.

Health

Health is the most unfortunate factor that forces poor to remain poor

Health platform is for providing health services.
The Provision of services includes medical, social and psychological services as well as, integration activities, care and nursing.

Clean water

It aims for long-lasting solutions to make clean water accessible for everyone, everywhere.

Clean water platform is usullay for improving access to safe water.
Also they help to provide effective sanitation and hygiene education to the world’s poorest people.

Plant

The greatest threat to our planet is the belief that someone else will save it.

Planting platform is for protecting, conservation and betterment of Mother Nature and Humanity.

Children

An honor and privilege to help the orphans of India in our own small way.

The vision of this platform for a happy, healthy and creative childhood for every child.
It works to impact the lives of children from low-income communities, enabling them to maximize their potential and transform their lives.

Women

Gives livelihood opportunities to women in need.

It works towards ensuring a life free from inequality, cruelty and discrimination for every woman
And aims to provide women with a platform to effectively get started with their business.

Elderly

A generous help from you can go a long way in bringing cheer and joy to the lives of elders.

This platform try to help elderly and making sure that they are taken care of in their old age.
It helps senior citizens lead active lives through various productive ageing and social support services.

Education

Education is key to self-development and empowerment

Helps to spread awareness about the importance of education and enroll as many children as possible so they can develop themselves.

Differently abled

Donate to help the poor differently-abled so that they overcome the disability and live a normal life.

Help to ensure that people with disabilities have equal opportunities.
Having a disability shouldn’t exclude someone from the opportunity to be independent.

Development

Give a man fish and you feed him for a day.
Teach a man to fish and you feed him for life.

It works among poor, orphaned and underprivileged people, people with HIV and abandoned, deserted women like widows, helpless and resource poor farmers to ensure their rights and development. They also provide relief and rehabilitation to victims of disaster and train them on disaster preparedness.
